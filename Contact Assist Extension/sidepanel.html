Core Functionality

This extension enhances Google Voice and Five9 by automatically replacing phone numbers with contact names from your Dataverse list.

  • Automatic Detection: The extension actively scans for phone numbers.
  • Name Replacement: Matches numbers to your list and displays names.
  • Remote Dialing: Click the main action button to dial.

Home Tab

  • Active Contact: Shows the currently detected number/name.
  • Message Bank: Click any message card to copy it instantly.

Settings & Customization

  • Your Name: Replaces {userName} in templates.
  • Custom Messages: Create templates using dynamic placeholders.
  • Pinning: Right-click a message in the bank to Pin it to the top.
  • Updating: Copy JSON data to clipboard, then click "Update Master List".
